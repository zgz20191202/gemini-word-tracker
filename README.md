# Gemini Word Tracker 🚀

**Track your daily AI usage and compare it to reading books!**

Gemini Word Tracker is a lightweight, privacy-focused Chrome Extension that measures how many words you **Ask** and **Read** on [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/). 

Perfect for writers, researchers, students, and AI power users who want to visualize their digital reading and writing volume, optimize their AI prompting, and track productivity.

---

### 🌟 Key Features
- **Smart Counting:** Counts words only after you send a message. No ghost counts from typing or deleting.
- **Accurate Media Handling:** Intelligently ignores non-text inputs and outputs (like dragged-and-dropped files, generated images, videos, and attachments). Only your actual text words are counted!
- **Real-Time Tracking:** Watch your word count climb as Gemini generates its response.
- **Multilingual Support:** Accurate word counting for **English, Chinese (中文), Japanese (日本語), and Korean (한국어)**.
- **Fun Analogies:** Compares your daily reading to the length of *Harry Potter 1* (approx. 76,944 words).
- **Privacy First:** 🔒 **No data leaves your device.** No chat content is stored. All counts are saved locally in your browser.

### 🛠️ How to Install
1. **Clone or Download** this repository to your local machine.
2. Go to `chrome://extensions/` in your Google Chrome browser.
3. Enable **Developer mode** (toggle in the top right corner).
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the folder containing this extension.
5. Refresh your [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/) tab and start chatting!

### 📊 Why use Gemini Word Tracker?
Ever wondered how much you actually "read" during an AI session? Use this tool to:
- Track your writing productivity and AI interaction volume.
- Measure your AI response reading habits over time.
- Gamify your learning and daily workflow with book analogies.
- Keep your data safe with a 100% local, privacy-first architecture.
