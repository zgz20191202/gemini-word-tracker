# Gemini Word Tracker 🚀

**Track your daily AI usage and compare it to reading books!**

Gemini Word Tracker is a lightweight, privacy-focused Chrome Extension that measures how many words you **Ask** and **Read** on [gemini.google.com](https://gemini.google.com/). 

Perfect for writers, researchers, and AI power users who want to visualize their digital reading and writing volume.

---

### 🌟 Key Features
- **Smart Counting:** Counts words only after you send a message. No ghost counts from typing or deleting.
- **Real-Time Tracking:** Watch your word count climb as Gemini generates its response.
- **Multilingual Support:** Accurate word counting for **English, Chinese (中文), Japanese (日本語), and Korean (한국어)**.
- **Fun Analogies:** Compares your daily reading to the length of *Harry Potter 1* (approx. 76,944 words).
- **Privacy First:** 🔒 **No data leaves your device.** No chat content is stored. All counts are saved locally.

### 🛠️ How to Install
1. **Clone/Download** this repository.
2. Go to `chrome://extensions/` in Chrome.
3. Enable **Developer mode** (top right).
4. Click **Load unpacked** and select this folder.
5. Refresh [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com/) and start chatting!

### 📊 Why use it?
Ever wondered how much you actually "read" during an AI session? Use this tool to:
- Track your writing productivity.
- Measure AI response volume.
- Gamify your learning with book analogies.
